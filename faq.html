Service Details

What laundry services does Gharit offer?
+

Gharit provides premium laundry services including washing, drying, ironing, and dry cleaning with specialized care for delicate fabrics, formal wear, and everyday clothing.

How does Gharit ensure quality laundry care?
+

We use premium detergents, state-of-the-art equipment, and trained professionals. Every garment is inspected for quality and handled with precision to maintain fabric integrity.

Can Gharit handle delicate fabrics and formal wear?
+

Yes, absolutely. Our specialists are trained to handle silk, wool, cashmere, wedding dresses, suits, and other delicate garments with expert care.

What is included in the laundry service package?
+

Each package includes pickup from your location, professional washing or dry cleaning, careful drying or pressing, quality inspection, and timely delivery back to your home.

Do you offer rush or express laundry services?
+

Yes! We offer express laundry services with faster turnaround times for urgent needs. Contact our support team for availability and premium rates.

Are there any items you cannot launder?
+

We handle most garments professionally. Items with severe stains, fabric damage, or special care requirements need assessment. Contact us for specific items or instructions.

Booking & Delivery

How do I book a laundry service with Gharit?
+

Download the Gharit app on iOS or Android, create your account, select your laundry service, choose pickup and delivery dates, and confirm your booking. It's quick and easy!

How do I schedule pickup from my home?
+

In the Gharit app, select your preferred pickup date and time window. Our drivers will arrive within that window to collect your laundry. Real-time tracking lets you know when they're on the way.

What are the service coverage areas?
+

Gharit currently operates across Mumbai with reliable pickup and delivery services. Check the app to confirm service availability at your specific address.

How long does the turnaround time take?
+

Standard laundry service typically takes 3-5 business days from pickup to delivery. Express services are available with faster turnaround for an additional premium.

Can I track my laundry order in real-time?
+

Yes! The Gharit app provides real-time tracking for pickup and delivery. You'll receive notifications at each stage of your laundry service journey.

Are recurring scheduled pickups available?
+

Absolutely! Set up weekly or bi-weekly recurring pickups through the app. Gharit will automatically schedule and remind you of each service.

What if my delivery address changes?
+

You can update your delivery address in the app before your service is completed. For bookings already in progress, contact our support team for assistance.

Do you offer special request or notes on my order?
+

Yes, the app includes a special instructions section. Add specific care requirements, fabric sensitivities, or special handling notes for your garments.

Quality & Guarantee

What is Gharit's damage guarantee policy?
+

Gharit provides a comprehensive damage guarantee. If anything happens to your clothes during our service, we will discuss the issue with you, contact you promptly, and provide compensation to cover the cost of your damaged garments.

What happens if my clothes get damaged during laundry?
+

We inspect all items carefully before and after service. If damage occurs, our team will call you immediately to discuss the incident, assess the damage, and compensate you fairly for the affected garments.

How does Gharit handle stain removal?
+

Our specialists use advanced stain removal techniques for various stain types. We treat stubborn stains with care based on fabric type. If a stain cannot be removed, we'll discuss options with you before proceeding.

Are there any items with special care requirements?
+

Yes. Items like cashmere, silk, leather, wool, and embellished garments receive specialized treatment. Always mention special requirements or care instructions in the app notes.

What quality checks does Gharit perform?
+

Every garment undergoes inspection for cleanliness, proper ironing, color fastness, and any signs of damage. Our quality assurance team ensures professional standards throughout the service lifecycle.

How long are items guaranteed after delivery?
+

We guarantee our work for 14 days after delivery. If you notice any issues with pressing, cleanliness, or quality, contact us within this period for resolution.

What if I find an item missing from my delivery?
+

Contact our support team immediately with details of the missing item. We maintain detailed inventory records and will quickly investigate and resolve the issue with compensation if needed.
