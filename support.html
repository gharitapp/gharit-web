FAQ

How do I schedule a pickup?

Scheduling a pickup is easy! Use our mobile app to select your preferred 2-hour time slot, or call us directly. Our delivery partner will arrive at your doorstep with a branded Gharit bag for your garments. Same-day pickup available for orders before 2 PM.

What areas do you currently serve?

We currently operate across all major Mumbai neighborhoods including Bandra, Andheri, Juhu, Powai, Worli, Lower Parel, and South Mumbai. Enter your pincode in our app for instant availability check. Expanding to Pune and Delhi NCR soon!

How can I track my order status?

Real-time tracking is available 24/7 through the Gharit app. Receive push notifications at every stage: Pickup → Washing → Ironing → Quality Check → Out for Delivery. You can also chat directly with your delivery partner for ETA updates.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major payment methods: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, PayTM), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and cash on delivery. All digital payments are encrypted and PCI-DSS compliant for your security.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancel or modify free before pickup. After pickup, changes depend on processing stage. Contact us immediately at +91 882-8244-356 or via app chat. If we've started processing, a nominal fee may apply. Your satisfaction is our priority.
