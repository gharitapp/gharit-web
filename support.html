Frequently Asked Questions

You can schedule a pickup through our mobile app by selecting your preferred time slot and adding your laundry details. We offer same-day and next-day pickup options.

We currently serve major metropolitan areas. Please check our app or website for specific coverage in your location.

You can track your order in real-time through our mobile app. You'll receive notifications for pickup, processing, and delivery updates.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, digital wallets, and cash on delivery for your convenience.

You can cancel or modify your order through the app before our team picks up your laundry. After pickup, modifications may not be possible.
