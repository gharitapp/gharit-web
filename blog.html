Revolutionizing laundry care across India. Premium service meets modern convenience.

February 22, 2025 — Today marks a significant milestone for us at Gharit. After months of relentless preparation, testing, and fine-tuning, we're officially launching our premium laundry service app to the public.

This isn't just another laundry service; it's a complete reimagining of how care, convenience, and quality can come together to serve modern Indian households.

We started Gharit with a simple observation: despite living in an age of on-demand everything, laundry remained a persistent pain point for millions of Indians. Whether it's the busy professional who doesn't have time to visit the dhobi, the meticulous homemaker who worries about fabric care, or the young urbanite who values sustainability—everyone deserved a better solution.

The Gharit team celebrating launch day at our Mumbai headquarters

Why Gharit? Why Now?

The laundry industry in India is fragmented, unorganized, and often unreliable. We saw an opportunity to bring the same level of sophistication and customer-centricity that we've come to expect from food delivery and ride-hailing apps to the world of fabric care.

Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with traditional expertise. Every garment that comes through our doors is treated with the meticulous attention it deserves—whether it's a everyday cotton t-shirt or a delicate silk saree passed down through generations.

We didn't just want to clean clothes. We wanted to give people back their time while ensuring their most cherished garments receive premium care.

What Makes Us Different

  • Premium Care Standards: We use eco-friendly detergents and advanced cleaning techniques that are gentle on fabrics and the environment.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Know exactly where your clothes are at every step of the journey.
  • 24-Hour Turnaround: Schedule by 9 AM, receive by 9 PM the next day.
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden charges. What you see is what you pay.
  • Trained Professionals: Our cleaning experts undergo rigorous training in fabric care and stain removal.

The Journey Ahead

Launching in 2024 was just the beginning. As of February 22, 2025, our app is officially live and serving customers across Mumbai, with plans to expand to 50+ cities by the end of the year. We're not just building a service; we're building a movement toward smarter, more sustainable fabric care.

Our eco-friendly cleaning process uses 40% less water than traditional methods

Our vision extends beyond clean clothes. We're committed to water conservation, reducing chemical usage, and creating employment opportunities for thousands of skilled workers across India. Every order placed on Gharit contributes to this larger mission.

Join the Revolution

We invite you to experience the Gharit difference. Download our app today and enjoy your first pickup on us. Whether you're looking to free up your weekends, ensure your workwear always looks crisp, or simply want the peace of mind that comes with professional care—we're here for you.

To our early supporters, beta testers, and the incredible team that made this possible: thank you. This is just the beginning of India's laundry revolution.

Here's to fresh beginnings,
The Gharit Team
